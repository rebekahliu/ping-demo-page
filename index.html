Ping makes location sharing easier, faster, and more private, all at once.

The Problem

In our day to day lives, we often want instant access to friends' locations, but without invading privacy. There's a need for an app that implements general purpose location-sharing without letting others abuse their access.

Our Solution

Ping allows you to access friends' locations 24/7. But due to the reciprocal location sharing, users can't abuse the feature. By alerting people when you've pinged them, Ping makes sure users are only requesting location info when they have good reason to.

Instant Location Sharing

Ping automatically updates user location so you and your friends' locations are just a ping away.

Add Facebook Friends

No need to manually search for friends, Ping automatically imports and suggests Facebook friends who are on the app.

Custom Pings

Send customizable pings such as a "Home" ping to check if a family member is at home.

In-App Messaging

Continue the conversation of "where to eat" or "did you get home?" directly with in-app messaging.
